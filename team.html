Michael Helmreich
Senior Vice President

Michael Helmreich brings almost 15 years of experience in commercial real estate finance, underwriting, and capital markets execution. As Vice President and Senior Underwriter at Fortune Capital Group, he leads the structuring and analysis of complex financing transactions across multifamily, mixed-use, office, retail, and industrial assets nationwide. His disciplined approach combining rigorous underwriting with creative deal execution, has helped sponsors secure optimal financing through saving banks, agency lenders, CMBS programs, and private debt funds.

Prior to joining Fortune Capital Group, Michael spent more than twelve years at Meridian Capital Group, where he arranged and closed over 4.5 billion dollars in commercial real estate financing.

Beyond his institutional finance background, Michael founded and managed Pine Brae Holdings, a private investment platform through which he acquired and repositioned a seven-building multifamily portfolio in northern New Jersey valued at more than $30 million. This ownership experience gives him a unique perspective on value creation, investor relations, and asset-level performance; insight he applies directly to Fortune Capital Group's borrower advisory and underwriting platform.

Michael is a Certified Public Accountant and licensed real estate salesperson in New York. He began his career at Deloitte Tax LLP in New York, serving major financial institutions in the firm's Financial Services group. He earned a Bachelor of Science from Excelsior College and completed post-graduate coursework in Business Management and Accounting at Touro College.

Underwriter
Asher Spingarn is a real estate finance professional specializing in commercial mortgage underwriting and capital placement. His approach blends institutional-level underwriting with a personal touch, building lasting relationships across the commercial real estate landscape.
